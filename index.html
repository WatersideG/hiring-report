Signal 1: Tech Alignment (0-30 points)
Evaluates technical skill alignment with job requirements. Includes:

  • Marketing: Google Ads, Meta Ads, SEO, GA4, HubSpot

Signal 2: Engagement & Leadership (0-20 points)
Measures extracurricular involvement and leadership capacity. Components:

  • Club & Organization Involvement (0-6): Participation, commitment
  • Leadership Positions (0-8): Officer roles, project leads, mentoring
  • Team Participation (0-6): Collaboration, group projects, competitions

Signal 3: Experience Depth (0-25 points)
Evaluates professional and project experience:

  • Duration and relevance of past roles (months)
  • Quality of experience (internships, full-time, research)
  • Certifications and specialized training

Signal 4: Intangibles & Trajectory (0-25 points)
Assesses non-standard factors that predict success:

  • Work samples and portfolio quality
  • Career trajectory and growth potential
  • Dual-major bonus (+2)
  • Early application bonus (+1)
  • Demonstrated self-directed learning
Total Score = S1 + S2 + S3 + S4 (out of 100)
